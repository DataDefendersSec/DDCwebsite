MISSION

+

Since 2005, Data Defenders a project of RISEUP International have facilitated human rights projects in over 30 countries, pioneering systems that protect vulnerable populations, expand access to technology, privacy, freedom of expression, and education. Technology for positive social change. We work at the intersection of culture, data justice, and tech to advocate for fair allocation of resources that empowers everyone. 501(C)(3)Tax Id (EIN) 83-2368839

DOJ CYBER FRAUD INITIATIVE

+

The Department of Justice Cyber-Fraud Initiative uses the False Claims Act (FCA) to target government contractors who lie about their cybersecurity, putting U.S. critical infrastructure at risk. We conduct compliance audits of contractors, mapping to NIST 800-171, fedRAMP, and CMMC.

DIGITAL FORENSICS

+
