PRIVACY
POLICY

DCC is committed to protecting the privacy of visitors to our website, as well as our members and volunteers. We are dedicated to privacy as a human right. DCC has established this Privacy Policy to explain what information we collect through our websites and how it is used, as well as protections for our members' and donors' personal information.

01 — NO LOG Policy

In this policy, "DCC" refers to DCC staff, board members, cooperating attorneys, interns, volunteers, and consultants, all of whom are bound by law or contract to keep confidential information they receive as part of their assistance to DCC. We are a privacy rights organization. That means we hold ourselves to the standard we demand of others: We collect nothing, We sell nothing, We log nothing besides the site functionality and safety. We respond to messages and do our best to treat all people with respect. This site does not track you. There are no cookies. There is no advertising. We do not sell, share, or monetize any information about visitors in any form.

We are a 501(c)(3) non-profit in Delaware under RISEUP INTERNATIONAL EST. 2005 as a decentralized Human Rights Organization.

02 — WHAT WE COLLECT

If you contact us directly via email or Signal, we receive whatever you send. We use that information only to respond to you. Standard server logs (IP addresses, request timestamps) may exist at the infrastructure level via our hosting provider. We do not access, analyze, or retain these beyond what our host automatically manages.

03 — COOKIES & TRACKING

None. Zero. This site sets no cookies, runs no tracking pixels, and loads no third-party analytics scripts. The only external resources loaded are fonts and open-source JavaScript libraries (Three.js, GSAP) served from public CDNs.

04 — THIRD PARTIES

We do not share data with third parties because we do not collect data to share. We are not affiliated with any data broker, lead generation service, or advertising network.
